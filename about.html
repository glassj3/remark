WE ARE HERE FOR YOU

MISSON STATEMENT Our goal with Remark is to simply help you, those sufferung quietly or feel misunderstood. To create a safe and informative enviornment that guides you to answers through comfort and humor.

Remark is close to my heart, because I myself deal with depression and anxiety. I felt alone, and like no one understood what was happening to me or how to help. It took almost a full year of therapy to see that talking about it helped, it literally lifted the weight off my shoulders. That is why I created Remark. To help you all realize this step like I did and make your recovery.
